About GPX Water Mapper

This app helps you enrich a GPX route with nearby water points from OpenStreetMap.

  1. Load your route: click "Select GPX" or drag-and-drop a .gpx file.
  2. Choose a search radius. Only water points within this distance of your route are included.
  3. The map shows found water sources. Hover/tap markers for details.
  4. Click "Download enriched GPX" to save your original route with water waypoints added.
  5. Use "New" anytime to clear the map and start over.

Keyboard shortcuts

  • ?Open Help
  • LSelect GPX (open file)
  • NNew (reset)
